## The AI Features That Actually Matter

Let me break down what the AI can actually do, because this is where respond.io separates itself from basic chatbot tools.

**AI Agents:**
You can set these up from templates (Sales Agent, Support Agent, Receptionist) or build custom ones from scratch. They learn from your business documents, website content, help center, PDFs, whatever you upload. Then they answer customer questions in a natural, conversational way.

But here's the important part: they don't just answer questions. They can:
- Qualify leads based on budget, timeline, or specific criteria
- Update your CRM automatically with collected information  
- Book appointments and send calendar links
- Share payment links for purchases
- Route conversations to the right team based on intent
- Handle voice notes and audio messages (huge for regions where people prefer voice)
- Work in multiple languages

**AI Objectives:**
These are workflow steps that collect information naturally. Customer says "My name is Sarah, my email is sarah@email.com, and I'd like to book a demo." The AI extracts all that data and passes it to the next step automatically. No rigid forms, no awkward prompts.

**Human Takeover:**
When the AI can't handle something or a customer explicitly asks for a human, there's a smooth handoff. Your team can jump in anytime, and the AI stops responding. The customer never feels stuck talking to a bot that doesn't understand them.
